More about Usagi Collective


When was Usagi collective created?

Usagi Collective was devised on 03/04/20, but was Officially Founded on 03/05/20 by RØŹÚÚ and Co-Founded by Øff Lyne.

The Idea!

RØŹÚÚ: "I came up with the idea of this collective because of one of our former producers, PHARAØH. He was asked to send a song to someone in a "high label". This person rejected his song which was ok at first, but then proceeded to tear PHARAØH down and trash his music. I've always wanted to make something like a label but right then and there, I decided to do it. And to do it for artists that are unknown and people would look away from."

Origin of the name "Usagi"

RØŹÚÚ : "The word ウサギ (Usagi) means rabbit in Japanese. I was teaching myself Japanese at this time and for some reason, this word always stuck with me. Even way before This collective was even thought of, It stuck with me and I didn't know why or how. Like bro, I was forgetting simple words on the daily but not this one. But now that I made this, it makes sense now."
